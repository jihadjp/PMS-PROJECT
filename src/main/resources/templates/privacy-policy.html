Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 3, 2025

Welcome to the Payroll Management System. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal and financial information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

To ensure accurate payroll processing, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification: Name, Profile Picture, Phone Number, Email Address.
  • Employment Details: Designation, Department, Joining Date, Status (Active/Suspended).
  • Financial Data: Bank Account Number, Bank Name, Basic Salary, Deductions.
  • Operational Data: Daily Attendance logs, Overtime hours, and Penalty records.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used strictly for internal administrative purposes, including:

  • Calculating monthly salaries, bonuses, and tax deductions automatically.
  • Generating official payslips and salary sheets.
  • Tracking employee attendance and working hours.
  • Managing disciplinary actions and penalties.
  • Creating system reports for HR and Finance departments.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: User passwords are encrypted using BCrypt hashing algorithms.
  • Access Control: Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) ensures that only authorized Admins can view sensitive financial data.
  • Secure Storage: Profile images and database records are stored in a secured server environment.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to third parties. Data may only be shared with:

  • Bank partners for salary disbursement processing (if integrated).
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

5. Cookies & Session Data

This system uses session cookies to maintain your login state and ensure security while navigating between pages. These are temporary and are deleted once you log out.

6. Your Rights

As a user of this system, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal profile and salary history via the Employee Portal.
  • Request corrections to your personal information if it is inaccurate.
  • Download your monthly payslips for personal records.
