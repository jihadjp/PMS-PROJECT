The net pay is calculated based on Pro-rata (actual days worked) with Dynamic Overtime (1.5x) and 5% Tax:

Net Pay = (Earned Basic + Overtime) - (Tax + Penalty)

Calculation Example:

Employee: Mr. Karim
Stats: Basic 26,000 | Work Days: 26 | Present: 25 | OT: 4hrs

  • Daily Rate: 26,000 / 26 = 1,000.00
  • Earned Basic: 1,000 × 25 days = 25,000.00
    (Salary reduced due to 1 day absence)
  • Overtime (1.5x Rate):
    Hourly: 125.00 | OT Rate: 187.50
    4 hrs × 187.50 = +750.00
  • Deductions: Tax (5% of 25k = 1,250) + Penalty (0) = -1,250.00

Final Pay Calculation:

(25,000.00 + 750.00) - 1,250.00
= 24,500.00 BDT
